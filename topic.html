Chosen Technology/Topic

Humanity has developed the use of technology for their daily life. Throughout the records, technologies have become a method to controls society. However, because of this innovation, many people fall victim to a cyberattack. Therefore, the terms "cybersecurity" is needed to keep the data safe.

Essentially, the use of cybersecurity in the internet is used to protect the data from being stolen by the thievery. As Craigen, Daikun-Thibault, and Purse (2014) said that "Cybersecurity is the organization and collection of resources, processes, and structures used to protect cyberspace and cyberspace-enabled systems from occurrences that misalign de jure from de facto property rights. This is a serious issue about how cybercrimes are influencing across every possible aspect to remain one step ahead of technological development.
